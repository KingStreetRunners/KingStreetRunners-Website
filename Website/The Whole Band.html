About the Band


It all started one night in the wonderfull town of Greenock. One very intelligent man had the idea to start a band. It was a day in the winter of
1995 when Colin rode into town on his donkey. Paul sat with the only instrument left in this one-quitar town, the Microphone, and ordered
a drink. He had barely set foot inside the inn when a local ruffian, Mark, took a liking to his taste in music. 'I do like your music style,' said
Mark, because he believed in speaking his mind. 'What's your name, stranger?'

From there on out, Mark and Paul were and are still are best of pals! Whenever KSR releases a new single, they always listen together.

All of the other members of the band were regulars at Marks bar. They had heard of Pauls unique style of music and had all taking an
interest to it. When Mark had introduced Paul to the others, he was scheptical. He didn't know how to feel about strangers, especially
not ones from the wild-west!

As you can most likely tell, Paul eventually gave in and accepted the new friends he had found! All of the people that are now part of
the band wanted Paul to teach them of his ways with music. Once they had all gathered their information, Paul sugested the idea of
starting a western band and so the KingStreetRunners was formed!
